Links and resources that may be helpful to the translators

Machine translation tools

SDL Machine Learning

This is a paid subscription software, which can help you translate faster. It is considered to be one of the most powerful, comprehensive, and adaptable neural machine translation platform today.


IBM Watson Language Translator

This company introduces a type of cloud, which you can fully adapt to your needs. It allows you to build industry or region specific custom models needed for your field of work and natural language processing engine is a powerful tool, which help many industries with translation across the globe.


Linguatech Personal Translator

This tool is a bit different from the first two examples. It may not include many languages but it certainly makes the experience individual and will easily adapt to your specific needs. It is an efficiant tool and compared to other programs of the same funcionality, it is also decently priced.

Translation and glossaries

United States Attorney glossary of legal terms

This is a short English glossary of term, which are used by attorneys of the US.


Glossary of English-Spanish legal terms

This is an extensive glossary of words used in the field of law. Its main problem is that it lacks sample sentences to demonstrate the context of the terms, which sometimes is essential since the same term in one language may have different words it can be translated with in another language. Otherwise, it's a good source.


TERMDAT

This is a database of definitions of term on various European languages. It can be of use to check certain terms and it allows to obtain results for several languages at the same time.

Proofreading and editting

Ginger

This online grammar checking program san help with finding grammatical, stylinstic and spelling mistakes.


Scribens

Apart from simple proofreading, this program has access to dictionaries to help you style and format your text according to your needs.

It will also highlight all the grammatical and punctuation errors and even give suggestions where applicable. Its beginner-friendly interface will help the process as well.


Small Set Tools

This is a useful editting tool, which can help you proofread your texts and also make them better with the word suggestions given by the program. Of course, like any tool it may have problems haldling long phrases and complicated grammar but it is good for the majority of paper work that is requiered from a translator.
