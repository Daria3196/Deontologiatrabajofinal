(Bank) Account Cuenta an arrangement made with a bank whereby one may deposit and withdraw money and in some cases be paid interest. He withdrew half the money in his account.
Él retiró la mitad del dinero que había en su cuenta.
Accounting period Período contable An accounting period is an established range of time during which accounting functions are performed, aggregated, and analyzed. An accounting period is the period of time covered by a company's financial statements.
El período contable es el período de tiempo cubierto por los estados financieros de una empresa.
Admissions officer Encargado (or oficial) de admisiones Advises applicants regarding admissions requirements, eligibility for admission, program options, residency requirements and other matters related to their admission to the University. Writing a Admissions Officer cover letter is your introduction to the hiring manager.
Escribir una carta de presentación de un encargado (or oficial) de admisiones es su presentación al gerente de contratación.

Los padres recibirán las compensaciones en nombre de sus hijos.
Billing statement (la) Cuenta de cobro, (la) facturación, (el) estado de cuenta A billing statement is a listing of the transactions impacting an account during a specific period of time. Billing statements are issued monthly at the end of each billing cycle.
Los estados de cuenta se emiten mensualmente al final de cada ciclo de facturación.
Business (el) Negocio The activity of making, buying, selling, or supplying goods or services for money William is in business as a shoemaker.
William tiene un negocio de zapatero.

I've got a pending business with a client.
Cease benefits Cesar beneficios Stop providing benefits due to their missuse. An employee shall cease to be eligible for benefits of this Plan at the earliest of the following dates.
Un empleado cesará de ser elegible para los beneficios de este Plan en la primera de las siguientes fechas.
Certificate of Citizenship Certificado de ciudadanía A document proving the citizenship of a person who was born outside the United States to U.S. citizen parents. A Certificate of Citizenship merely proves your citizenship status and is not a commonly accepted form of identification.
Un Certificado de ciudadanía simplemente prueba su estado de ciudadanía y no es una forma de identificación comúnmente aceptada.

Certification statement means a statement by which a physician or other authorized professional personnel attest to an individual's need for a specific type or level of coverage under the Medicaid program.
Declaración de certificación significa una declaración mediante la cual un médico u otro personal profesional autorizado da fe de la necesidad de un individuo de un tipo o nivel específico de cobertura bajo el programa Medicaid.
Citizenship Ciudadanía The state of being a citizen and accepting the responsibilities of it The requirements for citizenship in this country are strict.
Los requisitos para obtener la ciudadanía en este país son estrictos.
Civil status Estado civil It includes several situations, including being single, married, divorced or in a common-law relationship and whether or not you have children. Civil status ,generally speaking ,refers to the official relationship status of a person.
El estado civil, en términos generales, se refiere a las relaciones oficiales de una persona.
Common law Jurisprudencia, derecho consuetudinario The part of English law that is derived from custom and judicial precedent rather than statutes. Cuando no tengamos una ley establecida para consultar una demanda, nos fijaremos en la jurisprudencia.
When we have no established common law to consult in a claim, we look at the precedent.
Copyright Derechos de autor If a person or an organization holds the copyright on a piece of writing, music, etc., they are the only people who have the legal right to publish, broadcast, perform it, etc., and other people must ask their permission to use it or any part of it. The author has the copyright, so we needed his permission to make the film.
El autor tiene los derechos de autor, así que necesitamos su permiso para hacer la película.
Death certificate Certificado (acta) de defunción An official document that gives information about a person's death. I received my father's death certificate in the mail about a month after he passed.
Recibí el certificado de defunción de mi padre por correo más o menos un mes después de su muerte.
Decree Decreto A decision that is made in court. The judge issued a decree forbidding the defendant from leaving the state.
El juez emitió un decreto que prohibía al acusado abandonar el estado.
Delinquent taxes Impuestos atrasados; impuestos debidos y no pagados Property owners are required to pay their property taxes on time. If an owner fails to pay on time, the unpaid portion will be considered delinquent and incur a 10% penalty charge and, in the case of second installment, a one-time administrative fee. Delinquent taxes means the cumulative amount of unpaid taxes.
Impuestos atrasados significa la cantidad acumulada de impuestos no pagados.
Earned income Ingreso devengado Money derived from paid work. Earned income does not include interest or investment income.
El ingreso devengado no incluye ingresos por intereses o inversiones.
Earnings record Registro de ganancias Part of a person's social security statement which details the person's earnings over their lifetime. Earnings record is part of a person's social security statement which details the person's earnings over their lifetime.
El registro de ganancias es parte de la declaración de seguridad social de una persona que detalla las ganancias de la persona a lo largo de su vida.
Eligible Con derecho A person who is eligible for something or to do something, is able to have or do it because they have the right qualifications, are the right age, etc. We are eligible for a tax credit because of the energy-efficient furnace we installed.
Estamos con derecho de un crédito impositivo gracias al horno de bajo consumo que hemos instalado.
Family member Integrante de familia Family members include: Spouses and domestic partners. Children (biological, adopted, foster, stepchild, legal guardian, de facto or loco parentis) Parents and legal guardians (or spouse's parents). Here you can see all the family members.

Fees(professional charge) Honorarios An amount of money that you pay for professional advice or services. The lawyer charges a fee for his services.
El abogado cobra honorarios por sus servicios.
General tax revenues Ingresos de impuestos generales The revenues collected from taxes on income and profits, social security contributions, taxes levied on goods and services, payroll taxes, taxes on the ownership and transfer of property, and other taxes. General tax revenue is defined as the revenues collected from taxes on income, profits, and other taxes.
Los ingresos de impuestos generales se definen como los ingresos recaudados por concepto de impuestos sobre la renta, utilidades y otros impuestos.
Grant (government help) Subvenciones, subvenciones del gobierno A sum of money that is given by the government or by another organization to be used for a particular purpose. We have received a grant for our new project.
Hemos recibido unos subvenciones (del gobierno) para nuestro nuevo proyecto.
Immigration records Expedientes de inmigración, archivos de inmigración The records in this collection document the journey of your immigrant ancestor and their steps towards becoming a citizen of their new country. Immigration records are used to determine the gender, age, country of origin, and time of entry of all foreign-born lawful residents.
Los archivos de inmigración se utilizan para determinar el sexo, la edad, el país de origen y la hora de entrada de todos los residentes legales nacidos en el extranjero.
Income tax Impuesto sobre el ingreso, contribuciones sobre el ingreso Tax levied directly on personal income. The amount of income tax I have to pay seems to increase every year.
La cantidad que pago de impuestos sobre el ingreso parece subir año a año.
Inquiry Petición, consulta A request for information about somebody/something; a question about somebody/something. Kyle submitted an inquiry at the front desk.
Kyle envió una consulta al mostrador central.
Joint ownership Propiedad en conjunto, co-propietarios Ownership of a property or business that is shared by two or more people or organizations. My wife and I have joint ownership of our home.
Mi esposa y yo tenemos nuetra propiedad en conjunto.
Last name (surname) Apellido A hereditary name common to all members of a family, as distinct from a forename or given name. The Royal Family's last name is Windsor.
El apellido de la Familia Real es Windsor.
Lawful permanent resident Residente permanente legal Lawful permanent residents (LPRs), also known as “green card” holders, are non-citizens who are lawfully authorized to live permanently within the United States. Lawful permanent resident (LPR) is a status given to U.S. immigrants/non-citizens who can legally live in the United States forever.
Residente permanente legal (LPR, por sus siglas en inglés) es un estatus otorgado a los inmigrantes estadounidenses/no ciudadanos que pueden vivir legalmente en los Estados Unidos para siempre.
Lease Contrato de arrendamiento, arrendamiento A legal agreement that allows you to use a building, a piece of equipment or some land for a period of time, usually in return for rent. Karen moved into her new apartment the day after she signed the lease.
Karen se mudó a su nuevo apartamento el día después de firmar el contrato de arrendamiento.
Local government Gobierno municipal The administration of a particular county or district, with representatives elected by those who live there. Local government is the authority that determines and executes measures within a restricted area inside and smaller than a whole state.
El gobierno municipal es la autoridad que determina y ejecuta medidas dentro de un área restringida dentro y más pequeña que todo un estado.

Marriage certificate Certificado de matrimonio A document which certifies that a marriage has taken place, which contains information (as time and place) about the ceremony, and which is signed by the parties, witnesses, and officiant. I had to show my marriage certificate in order to change my last name.
Tengo que mostrar mi certificado de matrimonio para cambiar mi apellido.

Naturalized citizen Ciudadano naturalizado Naturalized citizens are foreign nationals who have become citizens of the United States after fulfilling requirements established by Congress in the Immigration and Nationality Act. I was born in Mexico, but became a US naturalized citizen by the amnesty offered in 1986.
Nací en México pero soy ciudadana nacionalizada estadounidense gracias a la amnistía de 1986.

La secretaria es notaria, por lo que puede certificar su acta notarial aquí en la oficina.
